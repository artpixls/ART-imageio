List of external binaries needed to use all the plugins
=======================================================

- exiftool
  from: https://exiftool.org
  needed by: all plugins

- ultrahdr_app
  from: https://github.com/google/libultrahdr
  needed by: ultrahdr

- avifenc
  from: https://github.com/AOMediaCodec/libavif
  needed by: avifhdr

- jxlinfo, cjxl, djxl
  from: https://github.com/libjxl/libjxl
  needed by: jxl, jxlhdr
